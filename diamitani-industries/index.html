Founder & Principal
PD

Patrick Diamitani

Production AI systems builder. Published researcher. Founder. I build the systems that let organizations run on AI — and the frameworks that let others do the same.

Fifteen years across entertainment, sales, and technology: enterprise sales at Uber, Upwork, and Asana; startup sales leadership at DreamItReel, taking revenue from $200K to $1.5M; a Techstars associate supporting three companies that collectively raised over $100M (two acquired); and a founding partner in Omicon Entertainment, producing 1,000+ shows for 80,000 students at $2M in yearly revenue.

Today I'm GTM AI & Automation Manager at Atlas HXM, leading AI-native efforts across sales, ops, marketing, and RevOps — my automations and AI builds have enabled over $16M in pipeline to date. I authored the ROSTR Framework, founded Artispreneur, Gency AI, and GPTPAT, built Your48 for Chicago's 48th Ward — and on the weekends, I make records.
